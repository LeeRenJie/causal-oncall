Causal On-Call converts the problem to a DQL plan, walks the Dynatrace topology, cross-references the last N deploys, runs the Davis changepoint analyzer, and produces a ranked causal hypothesis brief in under 90 seconds. It doesn't replace the on-call. It gives them a head start.
